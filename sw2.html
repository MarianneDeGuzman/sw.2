    NOLI ME TANGERE
    Maria Clara Maria Clara is a mestiza and the childhood sweetheart of the novel's protagonist, Crisostomo Ibarra. Rizal envisioned her as embodying "the purity and innocence of a sheltered native (Filipino) woman during the Spanish occupation."
    Crisostomo Ibarra Crisostomo Ibarra, full name Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, was a San Diego high school student.
    Padre Damaso Padre Damaso was the real father of Maria Clara and the former town curate of San Diego. The man tried to keep his daughter's marriage to Crisostomo Ibarra apart for the sake of Maria Clara.
    Kapitan Tiago Kapitan Tiago was a powerful businessman in San Diego and Maria Clara's father. Capitan Tiago struggled to obey the friars' will when he married his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra.
    Elias Elias was a fugitive living in San Diego. Enduring one tragedy after another, he began working to improve society. After his life was saved by Crisostomo Ibarra, he began aiding the young man, saving him on numerous occasions.
